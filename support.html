Frequently asked questions

How do I scan my whole Mac?

Click “Choose Folder” and select your startup disk or home folder. Because Diskly runs in Apple's secure sandbox, it can only scan folders you explicitly grant access to.

Is it safe to delete files with Diskly?

Yes. Cleanup moves items to the macOS Trash — nothing is permanently deleted, and you can restore from the Trash anytime.

Does scanning harm my SSD?

No. Diskly only reads file information; reading does not wear out an SSD.

What does Diskly Pro unlock?

Pro unlocks one-click cleanup (moving files to the Trash). Scanning and visualization are always free. Pro is a one-time purchase, not a subscription.

I bought Pro on another Mac.

Open the cleanup paywall and choose “Restore Purchases.”
