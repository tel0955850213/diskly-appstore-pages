Diskly Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Diskly does not collect any data.

What we access

Diskly reads only the folder you explicitly choose, in order to calculate how much space each file and subfolder uses. This reading happens entirely on your Mac.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not upload, transmit, or store your files or file names anywhere.
  • We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not collect personal information of any kind.

Cleanup

When you choose to clean up a file, Diskly moves it to the macOS Trash. Nothing is deleted permanently by the app, and you can restore items from the Trash at any time.

Purchases

Diskly Pro is handled entirely by Apple's App Store. We never see your payment information.
